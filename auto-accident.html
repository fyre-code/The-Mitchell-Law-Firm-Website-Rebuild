Steps to Take After a Car Accident

What you do in the hours and days after a collision can significantly affect your case. Joe recommends:

  • Seek medical attention promptly — even if symptoms seem minor
  • Document the scene with photos and video
  • Collect contact and insurance information from all parties
  • Record your symptoms and how you feel in the days following
  • Do not give a recorded statement to any insurance company before speaking with an attorney

Common Causes of Car Accidents in Florida

  • Distracted driving (phone use, inattention)
  • Speeding and aggressive driving
  • Failure to yield or obey traffic signals
  • Impaired driving
  • Unsafe lane changes
  • Poor road or weather conditions

When Should You Contact an Attorney?

You should reach out to Joe as soon as possible if:

  • Your injuries develop or worsen after the accident
  • An insurance adjuster has contacted you for a recorded statement
  • Liability is being disputed
  • Medical expenses are accumulating beyond what PIP covers
  • The other party's insurance is unresponsive or disputing your claim
