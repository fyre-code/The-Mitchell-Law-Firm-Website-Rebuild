Why Claims Get Denied or Underpaid

Insurance companies may reject or reduce claims for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Alleged lack of coverage
  • Liability disputes
  • Insufficient documentation
  • Policy exclusions
  • Misinterpretation of policy terms
  • Claims handling errors

A denial or low offer does not always mean the insurer is right. An attorney can help you understand whether the decision was justified under the terms of your policy.
