About Joe Mitchell III

Dedicated to serving injured individuals in Brevard County and throughout Florida for over 30 years.

Our Story

The Mitchell Law Firm was established in Brevard County by Joe Mitchell Jr., who built the practice on three enduring principles: preparation, honesty, and doing what is right for clients. His reputation for integrity and professionalism became the foundation upon which the firm was built.

Today, his son Joe Mitchell III carries that legacy forward. Licensed as an attorney since 1995 and earning Florida Bar Board Certification in Civil Trial Law, Joe III has dedicated his career to the same values his father instilled — while bringing decades of his own courtroom experience to every case he handles.

From one generation to the next, The Mitchell Law Firm remains committed to the people of Brevard County and beyond who turn to us after an accident or injury has upended their lives.

About Joe Mitchell III

Joe Mitchell III is a Florida Bar Board Certified Civil Trial Lawyer — a distinction held by fewer than 7% of licensed Florida attorneys. He has been representing clients in personal injury, wrongful death, insurance disputes, and complex civil litigation since 1995.

Joe is known for his clear communication and thorough case preparation. He takes the time to listen to each client, explain their options in plain language, and develop a case strategy built on the specific facts that matter most. He carries forward his father's commitment to integrity and professionalism in every matter he handles.

Joe works exclusively on a contingency fee basis — you pay no upfront legal fees. He only collects a fee when he recovers for you.

Our Approach

Legal issues can be overwhelming. At The Mitchell Law Firm, we focus first on listening — understanding exactly what happened, what you're going through, and what outcome matters most to you.

From there, Joe takes the time to explain your options in plain language, without jargon, so you can make informed decisions about your case. No two cases are alike, and Joe builds every strategy around the specific facts of your situation — not a one-size-fits-all template.

Throughout the process, Joe remains accessible and communicative, keeping you informed at every stage. Clear communication isn't just a courtesy — it's a cornerstone of how this firm operates.

Who We Serve

The Mitchell Law Firm represents individuals and families throughout Brevard County and Florida who face serious legal challenges following an accident or injury:

  • Individuals injured in car, truck, or motorcycle accidents
  • People hurt in slip and fall or premises liability incidents
  • Families pursuing wrongful death claims
  • Policyholders dealing with denied, delayed, or underpaid insurance claims
  • Clients involved in complex civil litigation matters
