Our Story

The Mitchell Law Firm legacy was founded in Brevard County by Joe Mitchell Jr., who built his practice on the values of preparation, honesty, and doing what's right for clients.

Today, his son, Joe Mitchell III, continues that legacy through The Mitchell Law Firm by representing people who have been injured due to the negligence, actions, or inactions of others.
