What Is Florida Bar Board Certification?

Florida Bar Board Certification is the state's highest honor for attorney competency and professionalism in a specific area of law. It is awarded by The Florida Bar to attorneys who demonstrate exceptional knowledge, skill, and experience — verified through an independent, rigorous evaluation process.

Board Certification requires attorneys to pass a comprehensive written examination, demonstrate substantial trial and litigation experience, provide references from judges and fellow attorneys, and maintain a spotless professional record. It must be renewed every five years, ensuring that certified attorneys maintain their commitment to excellence over time.

Why It Matters for You

When you are injured and seeking legal representation, the stakes are high. Board Certification gives you confidence that your attorney's expertise has been independently evaluated and verified — not just by clients or marketing, but by The Florida Bar itself.
